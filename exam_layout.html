G. Paragraph Writing _____/12

Topic: The Day the Earth Stops Spinning

In two paragraphs, write about 180 to 200 words, at least 7 to 8 sentences for each paragraph.

  • In the first paragraph, write about the immediate effects on temperature, the day and night, the seasons, and the animals and plants.
  • In the second paragraph, write about what humans can do if they are living in a dark side of the earth. Moving to the other side of the earth, planning to move to a new planet, or invent a technology to make the earth spin again? Explain what you think.

(1) Please double space your paragraph.
(2) Check your spelling and grammar when you are finished.
